Pack Accordingly

One of most important considerations when it comes to traveling to Las Vegas is to pack accordingly. Here are a few tips:

  1. Bring appropriate clothing. It's hot in the summer and cold in the winter!
  2. Pack sunscreen
  3. Carry extra water and stay hydrated
  4. Bring comfortable shoes - you'll be walking a lot!
